$$ I_1 = 4 + 0.8I_2 $$

$$ 80(4 + 0.8I_2) - 185I_2 + 90I_3 = 0 $$
$$ 320 + 64I_2 - 185I_2 + 90I_3 = 0 $$
$$ 320 - 121I_2 + 90I_3 = 0  $$

From (3): 
$$ I_3 = 2 + 0.9I_2 $$

Substituting in (4):
$$ 320 - 121I_2 + 90(2 + 0.9I_2) = 0 $$
$$ 320 - 121I_2 + 180 + 81I_2 = 0 $$
$$ 500 - 40I_2 = 0 $$
$$ I_2 = 12.5 A $$

$$ I_1 = 4 + 0.8(12.5) = 14 A $$
