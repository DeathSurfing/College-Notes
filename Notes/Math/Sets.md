### Union
- Combining of two or more sets
$$x=A \cup B$$
$$\forall x\in A \ \vee \ x\in B$$
### Intersection
$$x=A\cap B $$
$$\forall x\in A\ \wedge \ x\in B$$

### Union and intersection repetition notation 
#### Union:
$$A_1\cup A_2...A_n=\cup^n_{i=1}A_i$$
$$\cup^n_{i=1}A_i=A_1$$
#### Intersection
$$A_1\cap A_2...A_n=\cap^n_{i=1}A_i$$
$$\cap^n_{i=1}A_i=A_n$$
