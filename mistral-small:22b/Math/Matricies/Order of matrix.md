# Order of matrix

## Order of matrix:

The order of a matrix is represented as the number of rows(m) & the number of coloumns(n).

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f
\end{bmatrix}_{2\cdot3} 
$$
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 4 \\
2 & 5 \\
3 & 6
\end{bmatrix}_{3\cdot2}
$$
