# Union and intersection repetition notation

### Union and intersection repetition notation 
#### Union:
$$A_1\cup A_2...A_n=\cup^n_{i=1}A_i$$
$$\cup^n_{i=1}A_i=A_1$$
#### Intersection
$$A_1\cap A_2...A_n=\cap^n_{i=1}A_i$$
$$\cap^n_{i=1}A_i=A_n$$
