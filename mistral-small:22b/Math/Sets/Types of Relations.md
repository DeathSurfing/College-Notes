# Types of Relations

### Types of Relations:
 Assume true for all cases:$$A\neq \phi , \forall \Bbb R\times \Bbb R $$
- Reflexive
$$\forall a\in A  \rightarrow (a,a)\in R$$
- Symmetric
$$\forall (a,b) \in R \rightarrow(b,a) \in R$$
- Anti-Symmetric
	- For all values $a\neq b$
$$\forall(a,b)\in R \wedge (b,a)\in R $$
